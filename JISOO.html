JISOO

         Kim Ji-soo (Korean: 김지수; born January 3, 1995), better known by the mononym Jisoo,
         is a South Korean singer and actress. She made her debut in August 2016 as a member of the
         girl group Blackpink under YG Entertainment.;Kim Ji-soo was born on January 3, 1995,
         in Gunpo, Gyeonggi, South Korea. She has an older brother and sister.[better source needed]
         Jisoo attended high school at the School of Performing Arts Seoul.
