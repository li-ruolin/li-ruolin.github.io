LISA

          Lalisa Manoban (Thai: ลลิษา มโนบาล; born Pranpriya Manoban (Thai: ปราณปรียา มโนบาล) on March
         27, 1997), better known by the mononym Lisa (Hangul: 리사), is a Thai rapper, singer and dancer
         based in South Korea. She is a member of the South Korean girl group Blackpink under YG
         Entertainment.
